Why Wrestle?

At Bonners Ferry High School, wrestling isn’t just a sport — it’s a way to discover who you are and what you’re capable of. On the mat, you’ll face challenges that test your strength, focus, and willpower. Every practice, every match, every drop of sweat builds not only a better wrestler, but a stronger person.

Wrestling teaches discipline, respect, perseverance, and confidence. It’s about learning to get up when life knocks you down and pushing through when things get tough. You’ll make lifelong friends, form unbreakable bonds, and earn every victory — together.

Whether you’ve wrestled before or are brand-new to the sport, there’s a place for you on our team. No experience? No problem. We’ll teach you everything you need to know. All we ask is that you bring your heart, your effort, and your determination.
